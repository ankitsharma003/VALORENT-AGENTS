Jett's

Jett's agility and mobility make her a favorite among players who prioritize fast-paced, aggressive playstyles. Her ability to quickly reposition using her dash ability and her high skill ceiling with her ultimate ability can make her appealing to players who enjoy flashy and aggressive gameplay.

Phoenix

Phoenix is another popular choice due to his versatile abilities that offer both offensive and defensive capabilities. His ability to heal himself, control areas with his firewall, and blind enemies with his curveball makes him a well-rounded pick for players who value versatility and self-sustainability.

Sage

Sage is often considered a staple pick in many team compositions due to her supportive abilities, including healing and crowd control. Her ability to sustain herself and her teammates with healing orbs, as well as her ability to block off chokepoints with her barriers and slow fields, make her a valuable asset to any team.
